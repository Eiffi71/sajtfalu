Not only as a unique initiative in the village itself, but also in the region, small-scale dairy processing of goat milk was launched in spring 2017 in Magyardombegyháza. Goat cheese has been produced in the village in the framework of a communal work program for a while, without having a proper dairy processing site. Thanks to a grant scheme financed by the Hungarian government, the village gained a 100 million HUF subsidy to build its own small-scale dairy processing plant.
